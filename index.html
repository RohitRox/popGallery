popGallery

Jquery Plugin for randomized galleries

Sometimes we want random items to be fetched from a list and just pop them, rather than a slide show. PopGallery is the right fit for such situation where we want random popping items.

Example 1: Without Any Fancy Transitions

CODE 
      JS:
      $(document).ready(function(){
        $('#gallery1').popGallery({
          speed:2300, //speed of change
          cut_out:4,  //number of items to show
        });
      });
      MARK-UP:
      <ul id='gallery1'>
